Description

Lab Assault is an action-packed shooter where players infiltrate a high-tech laboratory to eliminate an evil scientist behind deadly experiments. Guarded by futuristic drones and heavily armed soldiers, the lab is a fortress of advanced security and lethal threats. Players must fight their way through intense battles, take down the scientist, and collect critical samples to stop his experiments for good. With fast-paced combat, strategic gunfights, and a high-stakes mission, Lab Assault delivers a thrilling sci-fi action experience.
