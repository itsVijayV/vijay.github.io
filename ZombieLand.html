Description

Zombie Rescue is an intense survival game where players navigate a post-apocalyptic world overrun by zombies, tasked with rescuing stranded survivors and leading them to safety. Armed with limited resources, players must fight off waves of undead, strategize escape routes, and ensure the survival of as many people as possible. With fast-paced action, immersive environments, and challenging missions, the game delivers a thrilling rescue experience in a world on the brink of collapse.
