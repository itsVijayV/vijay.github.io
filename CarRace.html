Game Description

Vintage Velocity is a fast-paced 3D racing game featuring three exciting modes: Free Look, where players can explore the track at their own pace; Against Time, where they race against the clock to set the best lap time; and Against Player, where they compete head-to-head for victory. With smooth controls, realistic car physics, and immersive tracks, the game offers a thrilling racing experience for both casual and competitive players.

Key Features & Technical Details

C# Programming

Clean, modular C# code with optimized racing mechanics, efficient AI vehicle control, and smooth checkpoint and collision detection systems.

Dynamic Racing Elements

Multiple race elements with unique behaviors: AI opponent cars with varying speeds, sharp turns and track curves, boost zones for speed increase, and dynamic weather effects affecting vehicle control.

Progressive Difficulty

Increasing vehicle speed and opponent competitiveness over time, with boost systems and score multipliers to enhance racing performance.

Visual Polish

Custom city-themed environments, realistic vehicle effects, smooth animations, and dynamic lighting to enhance the racing experience.
