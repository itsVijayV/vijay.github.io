Description

Car Race is a fast-paced 3D racing game featuring three exciting modes Free Look, where players can explore the track at their own pace. Against Time, where they race against the clock to set the best lap time and Against Player, where they compete head-to-head for victory. With smooth controls, realistic car physics, and immersive tracks, the game offers a thrilling racing experience for both casual and competitive players.
